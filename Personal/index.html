Services

My Services

RF IC Design

I specialize in creating integrated circuits (ICs) for radio frequency (RF) applications. These circuits are used in wireless communication devices like cell phones, Wi-Fi routers, and satellite receivers to enable efficient transmission and reception of signals.

Ultra-Low Power Design Techniques:

I excel in developing electronic circuits and systems that consume minimal power. This expertise is crucial for devices that need long battery life, such as wearable devices, IoT sensors, and medical implants.

Mixed Signal Design:

My skillset involves designing circuits that integrate both analog and digital components. This is essential for applications where both continuous signals (analog) and discrete signals (digital) need to be processed together, like in data converters and sensor interfaces.

Analog/Digital Layout:

I am experienced in creating layouts for analog and digital components on silicon wafers. This involves placing transistors, capacitors, and other components precisely to ensure proper functioning and performance of the integrated circuits.
